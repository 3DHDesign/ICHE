About Us

ICHE, an institution backed by the Certis Lanka Group, caters to individuals with prior experience, offering opportunities to enhance their skills. It's particularly beneficial for those in technical or supervisory roles, or those looking to advance in their careers. ICHE's caregiving program covers practical skills, theoretical knowledge, and communication, welcoming both experienced caregivers and newcomers. Successful trainees receive an NVQ Level 3 certificate.
